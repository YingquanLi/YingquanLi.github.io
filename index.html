About

Researcher in wireless communication

Yingquan Li received the B.Eng. degree in communication engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2020. After that, he received the M.Sc. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) under the supervision of Prof. Mohamed-Slim Alouini at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), where he is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree. His research interests include wireless communication, localization algorithms, and joint communication and sensing.
